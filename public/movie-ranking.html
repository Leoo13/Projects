The best Movies According to Leonie

My top 3 movies of all-time.

500 Days of Summer

This is a story of boy meets girl, but you should know upfront, this is not a love story.

Armageddon

After discovering that an asteroid the size of Texas will impact Earth in less than a month, NASA recruits a misfit team of deep-core drillers to save the planet.

The great Gatsby

A writer and wall street trader, Nick Carraway, finds himself drawn to the past and lifestyle of his mysterious millionaire neighbor, Jay Gatsby, amid the riotous parties of the Jazz Age.
